date: "2020-08-12"
tags: ["Asana"]

If you use the "Search this project" widget (via the "..." link on the right-hand
side of the project view), you can search for:

- `"nobody"` to see unassigned tickets
- `"not me"` to see tickets assigned to anyone but you
