You can hide night-time hours in Google calendar

In the “Week” view of Google Calendar, it’s annoying that each day is listed from 00:00 onwards. Since I never have events that start before 8am, it’s creates a lot of dead space:

Fortunately, there’s a Google Calendar Plus Chrome extension that has an option to limit the working hours that are shown.
